How will they benefit our society?

Solar Farms benefit our society because they reduce our carbon footprint on the globe and give us a chance to slow global warming. So far our chances of stopping global warming are sinking because of the use of coal burning. Solar energy won't run out during the day so it is one of the most renewable sources we have. This is also beneficial for everyone on a farm or house a while away from a power station so that they can have power too.
Also these are used in town and cities. An example would be how we put solar panels on our roofs, the power from these goes towards our houses so that society can reduce power bills.
