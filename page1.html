Energy Transfers and Transformations

With solar farms there is also potential and kinetic energy. The potential energy is when it is stored and gathered in the solar panels. This potential energy is transferred to the inverter which is transformed into kinetic energy or electricity and go to a fuse box which connect the power made on solar farms to the grid which is used to power homes and lights.
