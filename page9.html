Which Scientists work on solar farms?

In the field of solar energy, you can either be a Solar Career Map, PV Certification, Solar Training Network or a GEARED Scientist. These careers all contribute to working with solar farms and the analysis of the outcome. Each Role has a important role towards solar farms due to the complex system of a solar farm. While all of these are interesting, a few I want to point out are:

Solar Career Map:

Additional opportunities for progress and promotion can be found within any given occupation on the map, and multi-sector pathways reinforce the idea of lifelong learning and the natural evolution of skills and interests.

Solar Training Network:

The network allows for greater connection between solar employers, trainers and training institutions, workforce investment boards, and trainees and job seekers in order to meet ever-changing market demands, especially in regions where solar energy is rapidly expanding.
