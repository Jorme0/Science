How does the energy transformation work?

1. Sunlight hits the solar panels, and creates an electric field.
2. The electricity created flows to the edge of the panel, and into a conducting wire.
3. The conducting wire brings the electricity to the inverter, where it is transformed from DC energy to AC energy, which is used to power buildings.
4. Another wire moves the AC electricity from the inverter to the electric panel on the property. which distributes the electricity to different parts of the house as needed.
5. Any electricity not needed upon creations goes through the utility meter and into the utility electrical grid. As the electricity flows through the meter, it causes the meter to run backwards,  giving your property money off your bills for excess generation
